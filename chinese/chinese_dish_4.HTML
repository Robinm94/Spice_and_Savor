MaPo Tofu

Total Time Prep: 25 min.
Makes: 2~3 servings
Print Recipe
Save Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g ground pork
  • 300g soft tofu, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fermented black bean sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chili bean paste (doubanjiang)
  • 1 tablespoon Sichuan peppercorns, toasted and ground
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Add ground pork and stir-fry until no longer pink.

  3. Add minced garlic and ginger, followed by fermented black bean sauce and chili bean paste Stir-fry for a couple of minutes.

  4. Add cubed tofu and chicken or vegetable broth. Simmer for about 10 minutes.

  5. Season with Sichuan peppercorns, salt, and green onions.

  6. 7Drizzle with sesame oil and serve hot.
