Beef Noodle

Total Time Prep: 30 min.
Makes: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 300g beef (brisket or flank), thinly sliced
  • 200g wheat noodles (such as lamian or udon)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 star anise
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Green onions and cilantro for garnish

Directions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pot and add star anise, minced garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until fragrant.

  2. Add beef slices and stir-fry until they turn brown.

  3. Stir in soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, and salt.

  4. Add water and bring it to a boil. Simmer for about 20 minutes.

  5. Cook noodles separately according to package instructions.

  6. Serve the beef and soup over cooked noodles.

  7. Garnish with green onions and cilantro.
