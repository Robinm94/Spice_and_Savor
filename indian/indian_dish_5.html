Mysore Masala Dosa

Total Time: Prep Time: 20 minutes, Cook Time: 30 minutes
Makes 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Oil
  • 2 Tablespoon Chana dal
  • 1 Tablespoon Urad dal
  • 8-10 cloves Garlic
  • 1 inch roughly chopped Ginger
  • 6-7 Kashmiri red chillies
  • ½ Teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • ½ Cup chopped Onions
  • 2 Tablespoon Tamrind pulp
  • ¼ Cup Water

Directions

  1. Add oil to a pan and allow it to heat up. Once hot, add urad dal and chana dal lightly fry for 15-20 seconds. Add garlic and ginger and fry till fragrant. Next add the onions and fry till they become soft. Add red chillies, turmeric powder and cook for 2-3 minutes. Allow this to cool for a bit and then transfer to the chutney jar of the mixer.

  2. Add the tamarind pulp and the water. Grind till it becomes a coarse paste. Add extra water if necessary. It should be a coarse and spreadable mix and not too runny so adjust accordingly.
